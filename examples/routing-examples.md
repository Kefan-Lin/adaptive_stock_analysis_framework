## Mixed Business Examples

### Amazon

- Primary: `analyzing-consumer-retail`
- Secondary: `analyzing-software-platforms`
- Reason: retail and marketplace economics still anchor the group view, while AWS can inform secondary KPI and sum-of-parts discussion.

### Block

- Primary: `analyzing-software-platforms`
- Secondary: `analyzing-consumer-retail`
- Reason: merchant software and ecosystem economics dominate, while commerce and payments exposure can inform monitor triggers.

### Berkshire Hathaway

- No single route is clean enough for a simple one-skill conclusion.
- Treat as a special situation and state that a sum-of-parts view overrides a simple single-industry path.
